CloudPSS is a modeling and simulation platform for the energy internet. It uses a completely self-developed electromagnetic transient simulation kernel and heterogeneous and parallel computing resources in the cloud. It can provide users with modeling and simulation analysis functions for a variety of energy internet such as AC/DC hybrid transmission grid, renewable energy generation, microgrids, distribution network, heating network, and so on.
